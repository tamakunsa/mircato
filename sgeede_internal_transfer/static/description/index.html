Advance internal transfer system for Odoo

User-friendly multi warehouse internal transfer module with security on both warehouse.

SGEEDE's internal transfer is an enhancement to the default Odoo's internal transfer module. By using this module, user can transfer products between multi warehouse with advance entrepise business workflow and warehouse authorization to ensure that the product was transfered and delivered by the responsible user.

Advance workflow for enterprise usage

Transfer are divided to Draft, Sending (When product was marked as send by sender warehouse's logitistic user) and Received (When product was confirmed received by receiver warehouse's logistic user). This module work best with Odoo's stock valuation, support backorder system, and transfer log.
